## Why the name 'Relativity' ?

* It sounds cool.
* Yes, I took is out of the 'Theory of Relativity'.
* However the main reason is more philosophical. A lot of aspects of life are relative - and it depends on our perspective how we want to see them. I feel the same is applicable to investments too. Good and bad investments are relative terms and might even be interchangable based on the people evaluating them. Hence the name 'Relativity' felt apt.
